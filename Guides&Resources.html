General Brake Replacement Guide

Replacing brake pads is a common maintenance task. Follow these general steps to replace your brake pads:

  • Raise the car with a jack and remove the wheels.
  • Remove the caliper bolts and slide the caliper off the rotor.
  • Replace the old brake pads with new ones and reassemble the caliper.
  • Reinstall the wheels, lower the car, and pump the brake pedal a few times to ensure firm braking.
